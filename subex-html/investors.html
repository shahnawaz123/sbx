ABOUT SUBEX

For over three decades, Subex has been a pioneer in securing and optimizing the world's largest telecom operations, helping to build the very foundation of the digital economy. Today, as that economy undergoes a fundamental shift from an age of information to an age of intent, we are once again leading the way.

We have evolved from being the guardians of telecom revenue to becoming the architects of intentdriven operations. Our focus is on empowering businesses to thrive in this new era by leveraging our expertise in AI, analytics, and security to create autonomous, efficient, and resilient operations. Subex is committed to delivering sustainable value by turning market disruption into tangible growth for our clients and our shareholders.

Subex has been publicly listed and trading on the Indian stock exchanges since March 2004 (date to be confirmed).
